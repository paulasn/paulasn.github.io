Imagine Dragons

My favourite band

Imagine Dragons is an American pop rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada. It is composed of Dan Reynolds (vocalist), Wayne Sermon (guitarist), Ben McKee (bassist) and Daniel Platzman (drummer).
It gained worldwide recognition with the release of its debut studio album Night Visions (2012). Imagine Dragons won two American Music Awards for "Favorite Alternative Artist," a Grammy Award for Best Rock Performance, five Billboard Music Awards and a World Music Award.
In May 2014, the band earned a total of fourteen nominations in different categories at the Billboard Music Awards, including "Top Artist of the Year" and
a Milestone Award, which recognizes the innovation and creativity of various artists across different genres.
By January 2017, Imagine Dragons had sold 9 million copies of their albums, and 27 million in singles.
